Bodybuilding Calculator Site Plan

Cameron Bunch

Overview

Purpose

An interactive planning tool to achieve various exercise results

Audience

This site is intended for exercise enthusiast, both beginners and experienced trainers alike. Primarily targeting beginners, the “purpose” page (as seen in the wireframes below) serves to lay out the three different major results of exercise and why certain modifications (increasing reps and sets) to the same type of exercise will yield those desired (and different) results. Young men and women training to join the military or to simply perform better in sports will benefit from using the Bodybuilding Calculator to format their daily workouts. Other trainers may already know how to achieve the desired results as found on the “purpose” page, but may struggle to transition from exercising with no weights to then using weights. The opposite is also true; individuals used to training with weights may find themselves up a wall without weights to keep up their routine. This is where the “equipment” page will layout the three basic categories of equipment options that most people have to choose from. The third page will be useful for both beginners and experienced trainers: “Muscle Groups.” “Muscle Groups” will continue to prove an enigma unless they are mastered and the only way to truly succeed in any field is to master the basics. Therefore, review of the muscle groups will make understanding of the equipment used and the result of exercise that much more rewarding for the committed athlete.
